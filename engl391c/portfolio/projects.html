Art Portfolio

Portfolio item 1: The Mortal's Music (Graphite Illustration)

This piece was also done as an art project for GCSE Art, a still life drawing with graphite pencil. Objects used in the piece were obtained from the environment in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates and my family's old stash of retro technology. The Sony cassette tap and headphones were old and no longer functional, whilst the dry desert hyacinth and animal skull no longer have life to them. I wanted to capture the fragility of those that are mortal, in both the living and non-living, as they are not timeless, only fleeting. However, the cassette tape portrays the lasting of humanity's art and culture, evidently in music.

Portfolio item 2: The Choice to Unlock (Monoprint)

This piece was done as an art project for GCSE Art, where I experimented with monoprinting. These locks were collected from a local recycling and clean up crew, and I washed and cleaned them to take photographs. The photographs were then engraved onto acrylic plastic to be inked and monoprinted onto canvas paper.

Portfolio item 3: The 100th Article Post for SHEQUALITY's Instagram

This design was done on Procreate for the SHEQUALITY Club at the Crimson Academy SHEQUALITY Blog club, where weekly articles discussing about modern feminist events and topics, which are posted on a website and advertised on Instagram. I was in charge of many designs for articles, and I designed this celebration post of the 100th article published.
