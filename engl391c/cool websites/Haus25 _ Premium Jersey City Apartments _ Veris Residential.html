    Frequently Asked Questions

    building related faqs

    lease related faqs

    What is the pet policy?

    Veris Residential communities welcome domestic cats and dogs of all breeds and sizes. Pets must be registered with the leasing office. All dogs must be kept on a  leash while in common areas throughout the community. 

    What is the smoking policy?

    All Veris Residential communities are 100% smoke-free. Smoking or vaping any tobacco or marijuana products in our buildings or common areas is strictly prohibited for the health and safety of our residents. 

    What parking options are available to residents and guests?

    Residents can reserve a space in Haus25’s on-site garage for easy and convenient parking. They may also request a guest parking pass, available through the community’s mobile app. 

    When was Haus25 built?

    Construction of Haus25 was completed in 2022. 

    How does a hydroponic farm benefit Haus25 and its residents?

    Through Veris Residential’s partnership with Green Food Solutions, residents have  access to a cutting-edge hydroponic farm that doesn’t just grow greens—it cultivates community. Residents receive fresh, locally sourced produce right where they live, reinvigorating the “farm-to-table” experience in a deliciously sustainable way. By growing herbs and vegetables on-site, we’re reducing food miles and  empowering our residents to embrace eco-friendly urban farming methods with  interactive community workshops.

    What apps can residents use to enhance their living experience at Haus25?

    Residents can digitally connect with building amenities and apartment features  through apps offered by ButterflyMX, Ecobee, Technogym and About Golf. For residential and community needs, residents can access the myVeris app. 

    Is Haus25 located near any public transportation options?

    Haus25 is centrally located between major transport hubs, linking commuters to the  best of Jersey City with quick routes to Manhattan. The Grove Street PATH Station and Paulus Hook Pier are within walking distance of Haus25, offering convenient  access into NYC and surrounding areas. The Hudson-Bergen Light Rail stretches from Bayonne to North Bergen, with local stops across Jersey City on its route. 

    Has Haus25 received any Green Certifications?

    Haus25 is LEED Certified®. It has also received the WELL Health-Safety Rating and  WELL Equity Rating. 

    Is renter’s insurance required?

    Yes, a minimum coverage of $100,000 is required. All leaseholders must be listed on the insurance policy. 

    Are residents required to pay a security deposit? If so, how much is it?

    Yes, residents must pay a security deposit between $1,000 and a month and a half of rent. 

    Which utilities are residents responsible for?

    Residents are responsible for paying their electricity bill. Water, sewer and trash are included in the monthly cost of rent.
