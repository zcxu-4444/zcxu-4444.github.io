Year 1: Yingluo enters the Forbidden Palace

It is the Qing Dynasty, the year of 1741. Your name is Wei Yingluo, 
a 16-year-old girl with a sharp mind and nimble hands. 
You have entered the Forbidden Palace to find your sister's murderer. 
They can be anyone, from a fellow embroidery maid to the
emperor himself. Your objective is to:
- Survive the palace
- Find your sister's murderer
- Avenge her and possible allies made along the way

In order to survive, you must monitor your attributes
at the start of every year or event. 

Your attributes include:
- Status (Your current position i.e. embroidery maid)
- Money (Currency you can invest to your attributes and for certain
choices within the story)
- Food (The lower this attribute is, the more likely you will 
randomly pass from a disease)
- Health (The lower this attribute is, the more likely you will 
randomly pass from a disease)
- Beauty (This attribute may affect your story)
- Compassion (This attribute may affect your story)
- Suspicion (How much suspicion people around you 
currently hold. The more suspicious you are, the more 
likely you will be found guilty in any conflict)
